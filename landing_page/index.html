Identifying the Problem

With the rising use of electronic devices in our daily lives, many users find themselves developing bad posture habits.

Poor computer usage habits can lead to spinal, wrist and neck problems.

Devising a solution

Using the densePose API, we are able to track user's posture in real time providing immediate feedback.

With a google chrome extension, we are able to notify you whenever you display poor posture.

Our vision for the future

We believe that with proper usage, we can significantly reduce back pain and other tension related computer injuries that affect many people.

We are constantly working to innovate and increase efficiency to provide you with the most accurate data.
